ABOUT ME

Introduction

I’m Alex, a frontend-focused developer who values clarity, structure and refined digital experiences. My approach combines clean architecture, thoughtful motion and careful attention to detail, with the goal of creating interfaces that feel both efficient and enjoyable to use.

My background in dentistry shaped the way I work: with precision, patience and responsibility. Today, I bring those same qualities into digital systems, building with React and Next.js in a way that balances aesthetics, performance and long-term maintainability.
